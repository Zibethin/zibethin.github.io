About Me

Users and impact sit at the core of the products that I create or contribute to, be it for a decision-maker in a large relief operation, a yearly board meeting, a newsletter recipient, or a person affected by a disaster.
Pragmatism and agility drive my ways of working, be it in a crowded office or remotely in a different timezone.
I am a creative problem solver and approach my work with a double diamond process whether I am designing a survey for a programme evaluation, a dashboard for a recurring meeting, a one-off analysis for a campaign or an internal multi-year programme for an organisation.
